Career Profile

Currently completing the last year of his Aerospace Engineering undergraduate degree, Burton is applying for grad schools to pursue his interested in trajectory design and mission formulation.

In January of 2019, he began working on the interface for the JPL trajectory optimizer MALTO, where he adapted the software to better suit users new to the elements of orbital mechanics. At the beginning of the 2019-2020 academic year, Burton took the lead position in his undergraduate capstone spacecraft design project. At the completion of the year long competition, his team took first place and is set to have their designs submitted to the AIAA Journal of Spacecraft and Rockets. In August 2020, Burton presented his research into broad trajectory search algorithms for multi-flyby trajectories at the AIAA/AAS Astrodynamics Specialist Virtual Conference.
